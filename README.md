# Heart-Disease-Analysis-using-Tableau
A Tableau-based data visualization project to analyze heart disease risk factors using patient health data. The project explores relationships between age, gender, BMI, cholesterol, blood pressure, smoking habits, and lifestyle factors to identify high-risk groups and support preventive healthcare decisions.

# Heart Disease Analysis using Tableau

## Project Overview

Heart disease is one of the major causes of death worldwide. Lifestyle changes, unhealthy food habits, smoking, obesity, and lack of physical activity increase the risk of heart-related diseases.

This project focuses on analyzing heart disease patient data using Tableau, a powerful data visualization and Business Intelligence tool. The project converts raw healthcare data into interactive dashboards to identify important risk factors and provide meaningful insights.

## Objectives

- Analyze heart disease patterns among different age groups.
- Identify major factors affecting heart disease.
- Study the relationship between BMI, cholesterol, smoking, and heart disease.
- Compare health conditions among different patient groups.
- Create interactive Tableau dashboards for healthcare decision-making.

## Key Findings

- Older age groups have a higher risk of heart disease.
- High cholesterol levels increase heart disease probability.
- Smoking is one of the major risk factors.
- High BMI is associated with increased health risks.
- Physical activity helps reduce disease risk.
